Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

This policy explains how danielecomoglio.com uses browser storage and cookie-like technologies.

1. Necessary storage

We store your cookie preference locally in the browser to remember your consent choice. This is required to keep your privacy settings consistent.

2. External media (optional, consent-based)

When you allow external media, the website loads:

  • YouTube embeds (youtube-nocookie.com) for video sections.
  • Bandsintown widget for tour dates.

Before consent, these embeds are blocked and replaced with placeholders.

3. What happens if you reject non-essential cookies

The site remains accessible. Videos and the external tour widget stay blocked until consent is granted.

4. How to change your choice

You can reopen the consent panel anytime by clicking Review Cookie Choices in the footer.

5. Cookie duration

Preference storage remains in the browser until you change your selection, clear local browser storage, or clear site data.

6. Third-party policies

For details on third-party processing, consult their privacy and cookie policies:

  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Bandsintown Privacy Policy
