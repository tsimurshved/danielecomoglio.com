Biography

New Album "Traveling"

released in November 2020!

Personnel

  • Daniele Comoglio - tenor and soprano sax
  • Andrea Pollione - piano and rhodes
  • Luca Meneghello - guitars
  • Carmelo Isgrò - bass
  • Rino Dipace - drums

A visit to Brazil in 2018 for a concert tour was the spark to start writing the music on the album. A journey into ten original songs that mix smooth jazz atmospheres with ingredients from Brazilian music, African music, R&B and, of course, jazz as a trait d’union.
